About Me



Hello there! Before talking about qualifications and professional abilities, I would like to introduce myself. I am El Kanfoudi Younes and I am from Morocco. Taking software & web development as a profession not only fulfils my pocket but also my heart because it has been my passion since my early teenage.

I believe that people should do things in which they are good at or in which their heart lays. That’s why I chose freelance web development as my career because I believe I’m good at this and I am always honest and I am never afraid of doing hard work.

I have finished my graduation from The University of (Ecole normale supérieure-Tetouan,Morocco) on Software & Web Development and I have taken many lessons on modern web designing from famous institutions. My education background allowed me to thrive in this career and my trainings on this have made me a master of this section.
